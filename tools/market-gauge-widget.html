Tredey Composite Gauge (daily, end-of-day). Six indicators are each normalized to a signal on a [−1, +1] scale: RSI(14) linear-mapped over 30→70, MACD histogram scaled to ±1.5% of price, Bollinger position re-centered to ±1 around the midline, % from 20-day high linear-mapped over 0%→−10%, % above 50-day SMA clipped at ±5%, and volume ratio vs 20-day average clipped at 2×/0×. The six signals are equal-weighted, averaged, then mapped from [−1, +1] to a 0–100 reading. Zones: <20 deep oversold, 20–40 oversold, 40–60 neutral, 60–80 overbought, 80+ extreme overbought. Timeframe is daily; intraday signals are not captured. Past readings do not guarantee future moves.
