Privacy Policy

Plain-language summary of what data this site collects and what it does with it.

Data This Site Collects

  • Analytics. Google Analytics 4 (GA4) tracks page views, session duration, referrer, and anonymized user counts. GA4 uses cookies and may collect IP addresses in truncated form. GA4 data is used to understand what content is read and how the site is used.
  • Advertising. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve display ads. AdSense may personalize ads based on browsing behavior on this and other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.
  • Server logs. The hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages) retains standard request logs for security and performance monitoring. These logs are not shared with the site operator beyond what is exposed through Cloudflare's standard analytics interface.

Data This Site Does NOT Collect

  • No account creation, no email collection, no login.
  • No trading account information. You never connect a brokerage account to this site.
  • No payment data. If a paid subscription tier is added in the future, payments will be processed by a third-party provider (e.g., Stripe) and this site will not store card details.

Cookies

This site uses cookies set by Google (Analytics, AdSense) and by Cloudflare for security and performance. You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings; blocking may affect how the site displays or functions.

Information we collect and how we use it

We collect two categories of information. The first category is information you provide directly: your name and email address when you subscribe to the newsletter, leave a comment, or contact us through the channels listed on the contact page. We use this information only to operate the service you signed up for — sending the newsletter, responding to your inquiry, displaying your comment if the comment section is enabled. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties for their own purposes.

The second category is information collected automatically when you visit the site: your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, time spent on each page, and approximate geographic location (country and city-level, not precise). We collect this information through standard server logs and through Google Analytics (a third-party analytics service). We use this information to understand how visitors use the site, to identify and fix technical problems, and to improve the content over time.

Cookies

The site uses cookies for two purposes. The first is functional: cookies remember your preferences (dark mode versus light mode, your last-visited page) so the site can present the appropriate version on return visits. These cookies do not contain personal information and do not track you across other sites. The second is advertising: Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads that are relevant to your interests and to measure ad performance. You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ad Settings page (https://adssettings.google.com/).

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies entirely may affect the functionality of the site (preferences may not be remembered) but will not prevent you from accessing the content.

Third-party services

The site uses the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy: Google Analytics (analytics), Google AdSense (advertising), Amazon Associates (affiliate links), and the email newsletter service. We do not control how these services collect or use data; please review their respective privacy policies. We select third-party services that publish clear privacy policies and that allow users to opt out of tracking where applicable.

Your rights

You have the right to: request a copy of the personal information we hold about you; request that we correct or delete your personal information; opt out of marketing emails (via the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us directly); and lodge a complaint with the appropriate data protection authority in your jurisdiction. To exercise any of these rights, email the contact listed in the site footer. We respond to verified requests within 30 days.

Children's privacy

The site is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to the site, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last revised. Material changes (changes that affect how we collect or use your information) will be announced in the next published post and, where appropriate, via email to newsletter subscribers.
