Privacy Policy

Plain-language summary of what data this site collects and what it does with it.

Data This Site Collects

  • Analytics. Google Analytics 4 (GA4) tracks page views, session duration, referrer, and anonymized user counts. GA4 uses cookies and may collect IP addresses in truncated form. GA4 data is used to understand what content is read and how the site is used.
  • Advertising. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve display ads. AdSense may personalize ads based on browsing behavior on this and other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.
  • Server logs. The hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages) retains standard request logs for security and performance monitoring. These logs are not shared with the site operator beyond what is exposed through Cloudflare's standard analytics interface.

Data This Site Does NOT Collect

  • No account creation, no email collection, no login.
  • No trading account information. You never connect a brokerage account to this site.
  • No payment data. If a paid subscription tier is added in the future, payments will be processed by a third-party provider (e.g., Stripe) and this site will not store card details.

Cookies

This site uses cookies set by Google (Analytics, AdSense) and by Cloudflare for security and performance. You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings; blocking may affect how the site displays or functions.

Third Parties

Third-party services used by this site:

  • Google Analytics 4 (analytics)
  • Google AdSense (advertising)
  • Cloudflare Pages (hosting, CDN)
  • OptionsStrat (affiliate partner; only loads if you click through)

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are in the EU, UK, or California, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data collected about you. Contact via the contact page.

Children

This site is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

How Long Data Is Retained

  • Google Analytics 4 data is retained for 14 months by default, after which it is automatically deleted. Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained longer.
  • Google AdSense data is retained per Google’s advertising data retention policy, available in Google’s privacy documentation.
  • Cloudflare Pages request logs are retained for up to 30 days for security and performance monitoring, after which they are purged.
  • Server-side state on this site: there is no user database, no login, no session tracking. The site is static files served through a CDN. There is no server-side record of who visited which page beyond what Cloudflare captures in standard access logs.

Sub-Processors and Data Recipients

The third-party services used by this site (and their data-handling practices) are:

  • Google LLC — Analytics 4 and AdSense. Data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. Google’s privacy policy governs the data: see Google Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare, Inc. — Pages hosting and CDN. Cloudflare’s privacy policy governs request-log data: see Cloudflare Privacy Policy.
  • OptionsStrat (affiliate partner) — only loads scripts or sets cookies if you click through an affiliate link. Once you leave this site, OptionsStrat’s privacy policy governs your data on their site.

Data Security

This site is served entirely over HTTPS (TLS 1.3) through Cloudflare Pages. No personally identifiable information is stored on this site. The third-party services used (Google, Cloudflare) operate their own security programs, described in their respective security documentation. There is no user account, no password, no payment data, and no trading account integration on this site, so there is no user credential surface area to protect.

Children’s Privacy

This site is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under 18. If you are under 18, do not use this site or provide any information through it. If we become aware that we have collected information from a user under 18, we will delete that information promptly.

International Data Transfers

This site is served from Cloudflare’s global edge network, which may route requests through data centers in multiple countries. The underlying analytics and advertising services (Google) may process data in the United States or in the user’s local region, depending on Google’s routing at the time of the request. By using this site, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your own.

Do Not Track and Global Privacy Control

This site honors the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal where it is technically feasible to do so. When a browser sends the GPC header, the Google Analytics 4 integration is configured to disable advertising-features data collection for that request. The site does not currently honor the older Do Not Track (DNT) header, as the DNT specification has been deprecated in favor of GPC.

This policy may be updated. Material changes will be noted with a “Last updated” date below. Continued use of the site after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms. If a change is material (e.g., adding a new data-collecting service or expanding the types of data collected), a more prominent notice will be posted on the site’s homepage for at least 30 days before the change takes effect.

Contact for Privacy Questions

For data-access requests, data-deletion requests, or other privacy-related inquiries, use the contact page. The site operator will respond within 30 days. If you are in the EU, UK, or California and you believe your rights under GDPR, the UK Data Protection Act, or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) have not been respected, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.
