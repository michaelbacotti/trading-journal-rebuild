Contact

Press, partnership, and editorial inquiries.

For editorial corrections, methodology questions, or partnership inquiries, use one of the channels below. For trading or investment questions, please consult a licensed professional — this site does not provide personalized advice and is not in a position to evaluate your specific situation, risk tolerance, or capital. All response-time targets below are best-effort and apply to business days (Monday through Friday, excluding U.S. federal holidays).

Editorial

Corrections, clarifications, methodology questions, or general feedback: open an issue on the project’s public repository or email the editorial team at the address listed in the site footer. Please include:

  • The URL of the page you are writing about.
  • A specific description of the issue (a wrong strike, a wrong P&L, an unclear paragraph, a math error, etc.).
  • Any source material that supports the correction (exchange data, broker statement, regulatory filing, etc.).

Substantive corrections are credited to the reporter (with permission) and added to the correction log at the top of the affected page.

Reader Q&A

The journal does not offer personalized investment advice and cannot evaluate your specific situation. For general methodology questions (“how does the playbook size a 0DTE vertical?”, “what does IV rank mean?”), the education section and the playbook are the canonical references. If your question is genuinely about the methodology as published (not about your own trading), the editorial team can route it to the author for a possible future Q&A article.

Partnerships

Affiliate, sponsorship, and content partnership inquiries: email the partnerships address with a brief description of the proposal. The journal is open to relevant partnerships that pass two criteria:

  1. The product or service is useful to the journal’s audience (options traders at the defined-risk / intermediate-to-advanced level).
  2. The partnership does not require the journal to alter editorial content or to promote products that the author would not recommend on the merits.

Affiliate relationships that exist are disclosed on the relevant pages and on the about page. The journal does not accept paid placements that compromise the integrity of the trade-log or playbook content.

Press

Members of the press may reach out for comment, background interviews, or fact-checking. Please include your outlet, deadline, and a brief on the story. Background conversations are available on request and are subject to the same disclaimers that apply to all content on this site (see the disclaimer). The author is not available for promotional interviews about specific stocks, sectors, or price targets.

Compliance & Legal

DMCA notices, takedown requests, and other legal correspondence: send to the legal contact address with the subject line starting with “Legal:” so it gets routed promptly. Please include:

  • A physical or electronic signature of the rights holder or authorized agent.
  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
  • Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing or to be the subject of infringing activity, with enough detail for the site operator to locate the material on the site.
  • Contact information — address, telephone number, and email address.
  • A statement that you have a good-faith belief that use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the rights holder, its agent, or the law.
  • A statement, made under penalty of perjury, that the above information is accurate and that you are the rights holder or authorized to act on the rights holder’s behalf.

Privacy and Data Requests

For data-access requests, data-deletion requests, or other privacy-related inquiries (GDPR, CCPA, UK DPA), use the privacy-specific contact in the site footer with the subject line starting with “Privacy:”. The site operator will respond within 30 days of receiving a verifiable request. See the privacy policy for the full data-handling practices.

What This Site Will Not Do

To set expectations clearly:

  • The journal does not provide personalized trading advice, stock picks, or price targets.
  • The journal does not respond to “what do you think about $TICKER right now?” emails. These are routed to the disclaimer.
  • The journal does not accept payment for trade-log entries, strategy articles, or playbook content. Editorial content is not influenced by monetization.
  • The journal does not respond to legal threats intended to suppress factual criticism or commentary. Such correspondence is routed to counsel and responded to on the merits.

Response-Time Targets

Best-effort targets, business days:

  • Editorial corrections: 3 business days.
  • Reader methodology questions: 7 business days (often longer if the question warrants a public article rather than a private reply).
  • Partnership inquiries: 7 business days.
  • Press inquiries: 2 business days, or sooner if a deadline is included.
  • Compliance / legal correspondence: 5 business days. Statutory deadlines (DMCA, GDPR, CCPA) take precedence over these targets.
  • Privacy / data requests: 30 days per GDPR / CCPA statutory limits.

Email addresses are available in the site footer. The site does not provide a contact form because there is no backend to receive form submissions; all messages go through email. Out of courtesy, please do not send the same inquiry to multiple addresses — one well-targeted email is more effective than several overlapping ones.
