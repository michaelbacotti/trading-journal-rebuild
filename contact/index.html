Contact

Press, partnership, and editorial inquiries.

For editorial corrections, methodology questions, or partnership inquiries, use one of the channels below. For trading or investment questions, please consult a licensed professional — this site does not provide personalized advice.

Editorial

Corrections, clarifications, methodology questions: open an issue on the project's public repository or email the editorial team at the address listed in the site footer.

Partnerships

Affiliate, sponsorship, and content partnership inquiries: email the partnerships address with a brief description of the proposal.

Press

Members of the press may reach out for comment, background interviews, or fact-checking. Please include your outlet, deadline, and a brief on the story.

Compliance & Legal

DMCA notices, takedown requests, and other legal correspondence: send to the legal contact address with the subject line starting with "Legal:" so it gets routed promptly.

Email addresses are available in the site footer. The site does not provide a contact form because there is no backend to receive form submissions; all messages go through email.
