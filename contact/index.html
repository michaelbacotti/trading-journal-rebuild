About contact channels

This site does not maintain a contact form because there is no backend to receive form submissions. All messages are routed through email. The specific email addresses are listed in the site footer. For fastest response, please use the channel that matches the nature of your inquiry.

Editorial inquiries — corrections, clarifications, methodology questions, requests to expand on a specific concept — should go to the editorial address. We aim to respond within two business days. Substantive corrections are appended to the bottom of the affected page with a dated correction note; minor corrections (typos, broken links) are made silently and noted in the next published post.

Partnership inquiries — affiliate programs, content partnerships, sponsored content proposals — should go to the partnerships address. We do not currently accept sponsored content or paid placements. We do participate in affiliate programs for tools we actually use; the affiliate relationships are disclosed on the relevant pages.

Press and media

Members of the press may reach out for comment, background interviews, or fact-checking. Please include your outlet, your deadline, and a brief description of the story. We respond to press inquiries within one business day when the deadline is within 48 hours. For longer-lead stories, we aim to respond within three business days. We do not provide pre-publication review of articles unless we are a named source; we will fact-check direct quotes only.

For podcast, video, or speaking invitations, please use the partnerships address with "media inquiry" in the subject line. We are selective about media appearances; we prioritize outlets that reach our target audience (active options traders and serious retail investors) over general-audience financial media.

Compliance and legal

DMCA notices, takedown requests, and other legal correspondence should be sent to the legal contact address with the subject line starting with "Legal:" so it is routed promptly. We respond to legal correspondence within five business days. The publishing entity is Dependability Holdings LLC, a Nevada limited liability company. Service of process should be addressed to the registered agent in Nevada; the registered agent details are available from the Nevada Secretary of State.

For complaints about content, please email the editorial address first. Most complaints are resolved through direct conversation. If a complaint cannot be resolved directly, the matter may be escalated through the appropriate regulatory channel depending on the jurisdiction and the nature of the complaint.
